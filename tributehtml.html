Dr. Dimitri Pitovski

A man who has dedicated his life to allergy treatment.

Here is a short timeline of Dr. Pitovski's Life.

  • 1965 Born in Bitola, Macedonia
  • 1985 Leaves Macedonia to study Medicine in America
  • 1990 Graduates from Wayne State University Medical School with a focus of ENT studies.
  • 1991 Gives birth to his amazing and most favorite son, Stefan!
  • 1993 Acquires a position at Northwestern University Hospital as a ENT staff physician
  • 2002 Accepts a position at Wake Forest Bapist Hospital in Winston-Salem North Carolina
  • 2008 Leaves Wake Forest and opens private practice in Winston-Salem,NC known as Allergy Centers of America
  • 2014 Opens a second private practice in Charleston, SC known as Allergy Corp Group
  • 2021 Opened up a third and final practice in Lewisville, North Carolina, serving as a family practictioner providing allergy services.
  • 2022 Continues to research innovative methods of allergy treatments by partnering with numerous physicians from across the country.

Dr. Pitovski's life and achievement are testimony to the far-reaching contribution that one man's towering intellect, persistence and scientific vision can make to human peace and progress.

--Jane Bradshaw (Allergy Specialist)
